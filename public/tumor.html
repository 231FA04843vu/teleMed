About Brain Tumors

Symptoms

Headaches, seizures, vision problems, vomiting, mental changes, hearing problems, and balance issues.

Diagnosis

MRI scans are the preferred method for detecting brain tumors, often with contrast enhancement.

Treatment

Options include surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and clinical trials.
